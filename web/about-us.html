GET TO KNOW US

We are a family-owned hotel with a long history with our small but luxurious hotel with a rich history stretching back to the 17th century. Guests enjoys the atmosphere plus there is a strong emphasis on tradition everywhere from the rooms to the exterior. We consider ourselves very successful, and especially during the summer since the hotel is often fully booked. Our guests arrive from all over the world to experience our luxurious and traditional accommodation.

Famous Hotel Guests

For dignitaries and celebrities traveling to the new territory of Denmark, The Overlook Hotel was the premier place to stay. Will Rogers and Wiley Post stayed with us just two days before their fateful flight to Barrow. Famous mountain man Bob Marshall stayed here as well. Perhaps the most notable guest of The Overlook Hotel, however, was famous artist Sydney Laurence. Many of Mr. Laurence's years in Denmark were spent painting in his studio, which was located in the hotel lobby, while he lived in an upstairs apartment. Mr. Laurence is most remembered for his beautiful paintings.

Facts

The Overlook Hotel is one of Denmark’s most reputable upscale hotel management companies. Since it was established in 1973, the hospitality firm has expanded its international presence and today, manages 2,000-plus reversvations in less than a year. With ambitious plans to further expand its footprint, the company is on track to meet its target of operating 125 properties by 2020.
